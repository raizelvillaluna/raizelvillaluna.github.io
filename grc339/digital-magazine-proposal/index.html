Introduction

Surpriez

Surpriez is a digital magazine dedicated to sharing the latest news and insights on blindboxes and collectible mini figures. From Popmart and Smiskis to Sonny Angels and beyond, the site covers trending blindboxes, the artists and designers behind the figures, and the culture of collecting. It also explores the deeper sides of blindboxes, including consumerism, sustainability, and the ways these toys may encourage gambling-like behavior.
