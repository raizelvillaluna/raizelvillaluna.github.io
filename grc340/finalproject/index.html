Helpful Study Tips & Planning

Learn how to study efficiently and Organize your tasks!

Pomodoro Technique

Work in focused 25-minute chunks followed by 5-minute breaks. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break. This method prevents burnout and keeps your mind fresh throughout long study sessions!

Prioritize Your Tasks

Start with your most challenging or important tasks when your energy is at its peak. Tackle the hardest thing first, and everything else will feel easier by comparison!

Take Regular Breaks

Your brain needs downtime to retain information. Step outside for fresh air, do some light stretching, or take a nap! Even 5 minutes away from your study materials can boost focus for hours!

Create a Distraction-Free Zone

Put your phone either in airplane mode or another room entirely. Use website blockers during study time and let friends know your focus hours. A distraction-free environment can double your productivity!

Design Your Perfect Study Space

Create a dedicated study area that signals "focus time" to your brain. Good lighting, comfortable seating, plants, and personal touches make studying feel less like work and more like self-care.

Review and Reflect Often

Don't just read! actively engage with material by summarizing, teaching concepts out loud, or creating flashcards. Review previous topics regularly to move information from short-term to long-term memory!
